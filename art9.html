Painter = Edouard Manet

Manet’s famous painting of Olympia, was not showcased until two years later after its completion. Regarded as a highly scandalous painting at the time for featuring an unclad female. The painting shows a bare-skinned woman who is being served flowers by her servant. During the exhibition, ‘Olympia’ received various threats due to its controversial nature. The Salon took additional precautions due to the security threats to the painting. Today this painting is at Musée d’Orsay, Paris, France.
